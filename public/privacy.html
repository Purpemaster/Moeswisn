Privacy Policy

This website does not collect or store any personal data. No cookies are used, and no user profiles are created.

External Services

  • Cloudflare: The website is delivered via Cloudflare. Technical data such as your IP address may be processed for performance and security.
  • GitHub Pages: Hosting is provided by GitHub Pages. Technical access data may be processed by GitHub's servers.
  • Radio Streams: When enabling radio functionality, audio data is streamed from third-party providers. Their respective privacy policies may apply.
  • Social Media: Social media icons link to external platforms (e.g., X, TikTok, Instagram). Clicking them may result in data transfer to those services.
  • Solana Blockchain: Donations are processed via the public Solana blockchain. Transactions are transparent but pseudonymous. No personal information is linked or stored.
