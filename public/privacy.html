Privacy Policy

This website does not collect or store any personal data. No cookies are used, and no user profiles are created.

External Services

  • Cloudflare: This website is delivered via Cloudflare. Technical information such as IP addresses may be processed for performance and security purposes.
  • GitHub Pages: Hosting is provided by GitHub. Server-side access logs may be generated, but no user tracking is implemented.
  • Radio Streams: When users activate the radio feature, audio content is streamed from external providers. Their own privacy policies may apply.
  • Social Media Links: Icons link to platforms such as X, TikTok, Instagram, Facebook, and Telegram. Clicking these may initiate data transfer to those services.
  • Solana Blockchain: Donations are handled via public blockchain addresses. While transactions are publicly visible and pseudonymous, they are not linked to individuals by this site.

Security & Anonymity

This website uses no analytics, no ads, no tracking, and no local storage. All wallet addresses are visible and optional. Donation buttons are passive and do not send any data unless voluntarily used by the user.
